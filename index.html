This dashboard simulates what an AI-powered Brand Intelligence system would look like for a Personal Care FMCG brand operating in India. It was built to demonstrate applied thinking at the intersection of marketing strategy, consumer segmentation, and AI-driven analytics — not as a theoretical exercise, but as a working tool a brand manager or strategy analyst could actually use.
📍 Domain: FMCG Personal Care — India
🧠 Frameworks: RFM, Psychographic Clustering, BCG, AIDA
📊 Output: Interactive Decision Tool
🛠 Built with: HTML · CSS · JavaScript

What This Dashboard Demonstrates
◈
Consumer Segmentation: AI-modelled psychographic and behavioural clusters replacing traditional demographic-only segmentation.
◈
Demand Forecasting: Scenario-based 12-month projections incorporating seasonal triggers and market shocks.
◈
Competitive Intelligence: Perception-price mapping and share-of-voice analysis to identify whitespace opportunities.
◈
Promotional Optimisation: AI-recommended timing and channel strategy tied to real Indian market trigger events.
Analytical Frameworks Applied
▸
RFM Analysis — Recency, Frequency, Monetary segmentation to identify high-value vs lapsed consumers.
▸
Perceptual Mapping — 2-axis positioning of brands on price vs. perceived quality for competitive gap analysis.
▸
Psychographic Clustering — Moving beyond age/income to values, lifestyle, and digital behaviour as segmentation variables.
▸
Promotional Response Modelling — Mapping channel spend to conversion probability by season and consumer segment.
Methodology & Data Approach

The data in this dashboard is AI-simulated and grounded in publicly available market intelligence from sources including Euromonitor, Nielsen India FMCG reports, Mintel Personal Care data, Kantar Brand Health studies, and industry publications. All brand figures represent estimated/modelled values for analytical purposes and do not represent proprietary data of any company. The purpose is to demonstrate the analytical thinking, framework application, and decision-tool design that would accompany a real AI-integrated brand intelligence system.
